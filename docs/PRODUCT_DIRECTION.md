# Product Direction

## Game Identity

Dino Outpost is a first-person browser defense shooter about holding a remote dinosaur research outpost for a short, replayable mission.

## Core Promise

- The player opens a URL and plays immediately.
- The run is short, readable, and repeatable.
- The mission should feel tense, not sprawling.
- The game should reward aim discipline, weak-point hits, and quick threat reading.

## What This Is Not

- Not an open-world survival game.
- Not a large inventory or crafting game.
- Not a multiplayer game.
- Not a cinematic story project.

## First Priority

Make one polished 90-second combat slice feel complete before adding broader systems.
