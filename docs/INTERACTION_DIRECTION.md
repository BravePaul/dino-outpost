# Interaction Direction

## Player Feel

The controls should feel immediate, stable, and easy to read under pressure.

## Required Feedback

- Fire must produce obvious hit confirmation.
- Critical hits must feel stronger than normal hits.
- Reloading must be legible from HUD and timing.
- Base damage must be clearly tied to enemy contact.
- Scoping must improve clarity instead of hiding the scene.

## Design Rules

- Keep aiming the primary action.
- Keep the target, ammo, and base state visible without clutter.
- Make enemy threat readable before the player is already losing.
- Keep end states fast to understand and easy to restart.

## Avoid

- Input that feels delayed or noisy.
- Feedback that only lives in tiny HUD text.
- Overcomplicated secondary systems before the core shot loop is strong.
