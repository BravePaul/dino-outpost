## Notes

- These are richer PBR-style assets than the Quaternius flat-color low-poly pack.
- The original downloaded glTF packages are kept under `public/assets/models/_downloads/polyhaven-dino-habitat`.
- Several assets are high-poly for a browser game. Use them selectively, instance where possible, and consider decimation or LODs before placing many copies.
